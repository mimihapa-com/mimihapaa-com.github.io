ABOUT US - MimiHapa MEDICINE

Welcome to MimiHapa MEDICINE

MimiHapa MEDICINE — as HOME OF MEDICINE — is Tanzania's number one free online resource for allied health students and professionals.

We were founded with one simple goal: to make quality medical study materials accessible to every student in Tanzania, regardless of their location or financial situation.

From Notes Za Wizara and NTA modules to NACTE past papers, Medical books — you will find it all here at www.mimihapaa.com.
